Vendor Panel UI/UX Project

UI/UX Design

Doha, Qatar

2021

About

It is a Vendor Panel, also known as (consumer's dashboard). Where consumer can upload his services, products & staff. Track orders, manage subscriptions, write articles, review financial data & can respond to the reviews.

Using the panel is just so easy. User just needs to pick an specific plan which suits him best. Submit the proofs about he is an actual consumer. After verified in 24 hours. He is ready to go!

Features

  • Clean dark mode UI
  • Simple and straight forward UX
  • Creative Widgets (Can be drag)
  • Pastel Color Palette
  • Flawless Interactions etc.

Problem
Solving

The client of this project was looking for the designer who can beat the old style boring dashboard designs (which doesn't looks designed btw). Who comes with something modern and easy to use design because the vendor's have to spend their tons of time. The approach was just to keep their vendor's happy with the end product. And I was so happy to be a chosen designer for them.

Now it's my turn to create things best. First I fully understand's the requirements, target audience, goals, site mapping etc by the project manager. Then jumped to the deep research process. After getting a propper understading of how the end product gonna needs to be look. I started making low fidality designs (Wireframes). Granted UX flow permission from the client then created the clean UI of this vendor dashboard. Followed rules of UI designing like spacing, hierarchy, contrast, consistency, accessibility, Interactions etc.

I let the team to visualize their expectations with that clean & Minimal Interface of the panel.
