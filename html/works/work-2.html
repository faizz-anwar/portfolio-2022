About

This project was a little bit different because I haven’t made the app design. My friend Harsh was designed this app in dark mode. Then after a month I think, I have to convert it into the light mode interface. It was really fun when you have to work on the design which is created by someone else.

Purpose of this app is to let you access the fitness related services, specialists, products etc near you. The app is available on Playstore & Appstore. But their is also website available.

This app is a part of the previous project where I was made a website to present this app. You can see it here.
